The Rival




Everyone believes that they are the Hero of their own story, but the antagonists are necessary too. You may not realize it, but you might be the driving force that pushes a hero to reach their true potential.
You are a bit cocky and arrogant, but mostly misunderstood.
Not many things in life can excite you as much as combat with a Hero.
When the time comes, you will join the fight against the true villain, but you will always have a burning passion to fight your rival.
