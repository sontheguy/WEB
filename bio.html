Bayonetta, also known as Cereza, is presumed to be the last witch after a war with the Lumen Sage clan.
Now suffering from amnesia, she must sacrifice angels every day to remain in this world.
Her signature "Witch Time" allows her to dodge while slowing down time and counter attack.
She is good at using several weapons, including swords, whips, RPGs, chainsaws, and musical instruments, as well as demons to finish off he
Despite the negative connotations witches have, she has saved her world from malevolent deities and time traveling messiahs.
She enjoys flirting with her opponents.

Vergil

- Might controls everything.

One day while Vergil was playing outside, demons attacked and he was separated from his brother, Dante.
Ever since that day, he has only one thing on his mind:
MORE POWER!
He will stop at nothing to obtain more power, even going to such lengths as opening a portal to hell in the human world.
Vergil often comes to blows with Dante over his refusal to acknowledge his demon heritage.
His father's sword, the Yamato, is his prized possession.
It is rumored that Vergil's obsession for power stems from his failure to protect his family, especially his mother.

Raiden

- Your memes end here.

Raiden, real name Jack was always dealt a bad hand in life.
In his youth, he was a Liberian child soldier, butchering his enemies so brutally he was nicknamed "Jack the Ripper."
He later became a mercenary and had to swordfight with the president on top of the World Trade Center.
After a series of unfortunate events, he is turned into a cyborg, enhancing his combat ability beyond any human's.
When he enters Zandatsu Mode, he can slice his enemies into millions of pieces with his high frequency blade.
Raiden uses his newfound strength to protect the defenseless and fight against injustice.

Ryu

Ryu Hyabusa is a man who has devoted his entire life to serving his ninja clan.
Physically, he is the peak of human strength and agility, being able to run on walls and slash through enemies with ease.
He is a master of ninjitsu and is able to use various elemental attacks.
He occasionally partakes in extreme volleyball when he goes on vacation.
